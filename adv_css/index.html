Gyorg

Link faces out against Gyorg, the hideous fish that dwells in the shadowy depths of the Great Bay Temple in The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask, link goes on his mission to save Termina. Even the most daring explorers are terrified of Gyorg because of its sleek, aquatic shape and razor-sharp teeth. Gyorg's unrelenting pursuit lends a sense of suspense and urgency to the action as Link makes his way through the dangerous waters of the temple, keeping players on edge as they try to outwit the creature and land the last blow. Gyorg uses a range of maneuvers in combat to impede Link's progress, such as quick charges and strong leaps from the water's surface. It is a difficult foe to deal with because of its intimidating size and mobility, testing the limits of your skill.
